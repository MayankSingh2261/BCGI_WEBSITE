About BCGI

Empowering American Football in India

Board of Control for Gridiron in India

The Premier Governing Body for American Football in India

Our Mission

The Board of Control for Gridiron in India (BCGI) is dedicated to promoting, developing, and governing American Football across the Indian subcontinent. We strive to create opportunities for athletes, coaches, and enthusiasts to engage with this dynamic sport while fostering a culture of excellence, sportsmanship, and community.

What We Do

BCGI serves as the central authority for American Football in India, overseeing all aspects of the sport including player development, competition organization, coaching certification, and international representation. We work tirelessly to establish American Football as a recognized and respected sport in India's sporting landscape.

Our Impact

Since our establishment, BCGI has successfully organized numerous tournaments, trained hundreds of players and coaches, and established a strong foundation for American Football's growth in India. We continue to expand our reach and influence across all states and union territories.

Our Vision & Values

Building the Future of American Football in India

🎯

Vision

To establish American Football as one of India's premier sports, creating a sustainable ecosystem that produces world-class athletes, fosters national pride, and contributes to India's sporting excellence on the global stage.

Strategic Goals 2025-2030

Our roadmap for the future of American Football in India

01

National League Expansion

Establish professional American Football leagues in all major cities with structured seasons, playoffs, and championship events.

02

International Recognition

Achieve full membership in international American Football federations and participate in Asian and World Championships.

03

Grassroots Development

Introduce American Football in 1000+ schools and colleges, creating a pipeline of young talent and future stars.

04

Professional Training

Establish state-of-the-art training facilities and certification programs for coaches, referees, and sports medicine professionals.

05

Media & Broadcasting

Secure major broadcasting partnerships to bring American Football to millions of Indian households and build a strong fan base.

06

Corporate Partnerships

Develop strategic partnerships with major corporations to secure sustainable funding and sponsorship for long-term growth.
